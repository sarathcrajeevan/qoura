In order to activate the fellowship, scanned copies of the following documents must be submitted for verification:
1. Candidate Data Sheet
2. Candidate Undertaking
3. Candidate Bank Account Details
4. HRA Declaration/Hostel Allotment Proof
5. Host Institution NEFT/ RTGS Electronic Fund Transfer Form

These scanned documents shall be duly forwarded and stamped by the candidate’s Ph.D guide/supervisor.
